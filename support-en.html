Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a box?

Go to the Boxes tab and tap the + button. Enter a 10-character alphanumeric identifier and optional position/dimensions.

How does AI analysis work?

Take a photo of your item, tap "Analyze with AI", and our AI will identify the object, colors, materials, and other details. Requires AI credits (10 free at start).

What's the difference between local and cloud storage?

Local mode stores all data on your device. Cloud Sync (optional purchase) backs up your data to Firebase, allowing access from multiple devices.

How do I generate QR codes for boxes?

Open a box and tap the QR icon. You can print the QR code and attach it to your physical box for quick scanning.

Can I export my inventory?

Yes! You can generate PDF reports with box contents and QR codes from the box detail screen.

App Requirements

Privacy & Security

Your data is completely private and secure. In local mode, everything stays on your device. With cloud sync, data is encrypted and accessible only to you. See our Privacy Policy for details.
